05 · Questions

What most operators ask first.

Can I pick just one service, or do I have to commit to the full engine?
Every service on this page is available à la carte. The Location Growth Engine™ bundles all of them at roughly 35% off the combined à la carte rate, but a single-service engagement is completely valid — we ship it on the same fixed timeline.
How is pricing structured? Retainer, hourly, fixed fee?
Every service is fixed-fee per scope. Ongoing pieces (Content Engine, Review + Reactivation) have a setup fee plus a flat monthly. No hourly. No percentage of ad spend. No surprise invoices.
Do you work with clients outside multi-location service brands?
Occasionally, but reluctantly. The stack and framework are engineered specifically for multi-location service businesses — med spas, DSOs, HVAC, home services, law firm groups, fitness franchises. Single-location businesses with plans to scale are on-ramp clients; everyone else gets politely redirected.
What if I already have a CRM or website?
We plug in. The AI Intake Agent and Review Engine can layer onto most CRMs (HubSpot, Salesforce, Jobber, Housecall Pro, Keap) though GHL is where the stack runs most cleanly. If your site is already strong, we can skip the rebuild and focus on intake + content + compounding.
Who actually builds the work? Is this outsourced?
Every deliverable is built in-house by Grwthly. No offshore content mills, no white-label agency partnerships, no subcontracted code. The operator who quotes is the operator who builds.
What happens after Day 90 on the full Growth Engine?
You either continue on an ongoing cadence (paid monthly, month-to-month, cancel anytime) or you take the keys and run the system internally. Full documentation and a recorded walkthrough are part of handoff.
