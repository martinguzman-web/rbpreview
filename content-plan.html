01 · Strategic Thesis

Not more content. Engineered content.

Every piece produced under this plan serves one of four outcomes. No piece is published that doesn't serve one. This is the difference between a blog and a growth engine.

The job of this plan is not to turn Roofboys into a media company. The job is to engineer four outcomes — each tied to a Location Growth Engine™ layer.
01

Win local search in twelve DFW cities so organic traffic replaces paid ad dependency.

02

Convert visitors with content that answers the exact questions homeowners ask in the final 48 hours before hiring a roofer.

03

Earn reviews and referrals through content-led follow-up, not random asks.

04

Compound authority so Roofboys becomes the name neighborhoods already know when the next hail storm hits.

"The DFW homeowner's most trusted answer on roofing decisions."

Not roofing tips. Not generic advice. The specific, operator-level answers a Dallas homeowner needs in the 48 hours after a storm, the two weeks after a denied claim, or the five years into owning a home.
