Questions DFW Homeowners Ask

Answers, no marketing speak.

How long does a full roof replacement take?
Most DFW residential roofs are done in a single day. Larger or more complex homes (tile, multi-pitch, multi-story) may stretch into two. Our crews arrive between 6–7am and are gone by dusk.
Do you really do 22-minute inspections?
Yes. If we don't finish the inspection in 22 minutes, the inspection is free and we still send you the full report. We time it — and we publish our averages. Current: 19 min 40 sec.
Do you work with my insurance company?
Every major carrier in Texas: Allstate, State Farm, Farmers, USAA, Liberty, Progressive, Nationwide, and most regional carriers. We'll meet your adjuster on-site and walk through every line of the scope.
Is financing available?
Yes. We offer 0% financing for qualified buyers through our partner lenders, plus extended payment plans up to 120 months. Ask during your quote.
What happens if I have an issue after the install?
Call us. Our workmanship warranty is lifetime and transferable. If we installed it, we fix it — no paperwork fight, no "that's outside scope." That's the whole reason we don't subcontract.
Which DFW cities do you serve?
Dallas, Plano, Frisco, Allen, Denton, Irving, Garland, Rockwall, Arlington, Carrollton, Fort Worth, Lewisville, and The Colony. If you're near DFW and not on that list, call us — we can usually make it work.
